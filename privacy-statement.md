# Privacy Policy

**Effective date:** February 27, 2026  
**App:** Localhost Port Scanner  
**Developer:** Nodera Labs  
**Contact:** nodera.laboratories@gmail.com

Localhost Port Scanner respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the app does and does not collect.

## Summary

- We do **not** collect, store, or sell personal data.
- We do **not** require account creation.
- We do **not** track your activity across apps or websites.
- All scanning is performed locally on your Mac.

## Information We Collect

Localhost Port Scanner does not collect personal information.

The app reads local system networking/process information on your device (for example, listening ports, process names, and process IDs) only to provide its core functionality in the menu bar. This information is processed locally and is not sent to Nodera Labs.

## Network Access

The app may send local network requests to `127.0.0.1` / `localhost` to test whether a local service responds as HTTP. These requests stay on your device.

The app does not send analytics, advertising identifiers, or usage telemetry to external servers operated by Nodera Labs.

## Data Sharing

We do not share user data with third parties because we do not collect user data.

## Data Retention

Because we do not collect personal data, we do not retain personal data on our servers.

## Children’s Privacy

The app is intended for general developer and technical use and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Security

The app operates locally and uses standard macOS APIs and system tools. No method of software operation is perfectly risk-free, but we design the app to minimize data exposure.

## Your Choices

You may stop using the app at any time by quitting or uninstalling it.
