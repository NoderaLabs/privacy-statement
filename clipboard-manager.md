# Privacy Statement

**Effective date:** March 5, 2026  
**Company:** Nodera Labs  
**Contact:** nodera.laboratories@gmail.com

## Overview
Clipboard Manager ("the App") is developed by Nodera Labs.  
Your privacy is important to us. The App is designed to keep your clipboard data on your own Mac.

## What Data the App Processes
The App may process:
- Clipboard text you copy
- Clipboard images you copy (including screenshots copied to clipboard)
- Screenshot image files from a folder you explicitly configure in Settings
- App bundle identifiers used for your "Excluded Apps" privacy list

## Where Data Is Stored
All App data is stored locally on your Mac:
- SQLite metadata database in your Application Support directory
- Image and thumbnail files in your Application Support directory
- User preferences in local system preferences/UserDefaults

The App does **not** require a cloud account and does **not** sync clipboard history to cloud services.

## Data Sharing
Nodera Labs does not receive, collect, or sell your clipboard history through the App.  
Clipboard data is not transmitted to Nodera Labs servers by default.

## Excluded Apps (Privacy Exceptions)
You can add apps to an Excluded Apps list.  
When an excluded app is active, clipboard changes are ignored and not saved to history.

## User Controls
You can control your data in the App, including:
- Turning clipboard recording on/off
- Deleting non-favorite history items
- Deleting individual items
- Marking items as favorites
- Configuring maximum retained items
- Choosing or clearing a screenshot folder
- Managing excluded apps

## Screenshot Behavior
If enabled, the App can set macOS screenshot target to clipboard.  
If you use file-based screenshots, the App can import screenshots only from the folder you choose.

## Retention
Clipboard history is kept locally until removed by:
- Your manual deletion actions
- Retention limits you configure in Settings

## Security
The App uses macOS-native storage and system APIs.  
No method can guarantee absolute security, but we aim to minimize data exposure by keeping data local.

## Children’s Privacy
The App is not directed to children and does not intentionally collect personal data from children.

The updated version will be posted with a new effective date.
